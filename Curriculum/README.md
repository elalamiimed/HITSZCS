# Curriculum

Welcome to the HITSZ Curriculum repository! This folder contains comprehensive course materials, resources, and documentation for students at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen (HITSZ).

### üéì Freshman Year
Contains foundational courses for first-year students. Each folder includes slides, homework assignments, model tests, and study materials.

**Core Mathematics & Physics Courses:**

- **Calculus** (5 Credits, 80 Hours) - An examination course covering fundamental calculus concepts. Grading: 20% Homework + 30% Midterm + 50% Final. Features rigorous problem-solving and skill practice questions similar to final exams.

- **Pre-Calculus** - Preparatory mathematics for calculus (20% Homework + 30% Midterm + 50% Final). Study resources and notes available through course materials.

- **Linear Algebra** - Matrix operations, vector spaces, and linear transformations. Includes 2024 and 2025 versions with in-class materials, midterm, and homework solutions.

- **College Physics IA** (Difficulty: Very High) - Covers mechanics (Chapters 1-10, midterm) and electromagnetism (Chapters 11-16, final exam). Grading: 20% Assignments/Quizzes + 30% Midterm + 50% Final. Attendance recorded via QR code; high failure rate requires careful attention.

- **Physics Lab IA** - Experimental physics laboratory work for hands-on learning.

- **Electric Circuits** - Basic circuit theory and analysis (40% Assignments + 60% Final Exam). Includes textbook solutions and course materials.

**Language Course:**

- **Elementary Chinese I** - Chinese language course for international students. Emphasis on writing and pronunciation. Multiple versions available (2024-2025) with structured lessons from different instructors.

**Physics & Engineering:**

- **College Physics IB** (Difficulty: Moderate, compared to IA) - Advanced physics topics including:
  - Waves (sound and electromagnetic waves)
  - Thermodynamics (heat transfer, latent heat, entropy)
  - Optics (refraction, diffraction, interference, single/double slit experiments)
  - Quantum Mechanics (Schr√∂dinger equation, wave functions, probability)
  Organized with topic slides, assignments, and practice materials.

- **Physics Lab IB** - Advanced experimental physics complementing Physics IB theory.

- **Fundamentals of Electronic Technology** - Covers both analog and digital circuits with separate folders for each. Includes homework assignments and comprehensive slide presentations.

**Computer Science & Programming:**

- **High-level Language Programming (C++)** - Programming fundamentals taught by Prof. Dr. Yitian Shao. Critical for success in Data Structures & Algorithms. Emphasizes writing code by hand for exams. Includes 2024-2025 lectures and lab work.

- **Data Structures** - Technically "Data Structures and Algorithms" course covering:
  - C++ data types and arrays
  - Stacks, queues, linked lists
  - Sorting algorithms (Bubble, Insertion, Selection)
  - Advanced algorithms (KMP, linked list manipulation)

- **Computer Networks** - Networking fundamentals and protocols. Includes resources for certifications like TryHackMe and Cisco Networking Academy for extended learning.

**Mathematics:**

- **Probability and Statistics** - Statistical methods and probability theory. Essential PDFs and lookup tables available in course materials.

**Language Course:**

- **Intermediate Chinese I** - Continuation of Chinese language studies for international students.

**Other:**

- **Life And Health Science** - Health and biological sciences course materials.

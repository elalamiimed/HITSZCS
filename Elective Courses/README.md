### Chinese Path to Modernization

**Course Details:**
- **Credits:** 2
- **Hours:** 32 hours
- **Grading:** 50% Group Presentation + 50% Report Essay

**Instructors:**
- **Guo Laoshi** - Teaches from a practical perspective with organized field trips to companies
- **Zhang Laoshi** - Focuses on theoretical foundations (School of Management professor)

**Course Features:**
- Organized trips to companies and practical field sites
- Opportunity to connect with School of Management professors
- Access to Research Competitions for valuable experience
- Both practical and theoretical approaches to understanding China's modernization

**Tips for Success:**
- Pay careful attention to slides and take detailed notes
- Zhang Laoshi awards extra points for comprehensive notes submitted with your report
- **Important:** Include your picture with your report (Zhang Laoshi values participation and engagement)
- Actively participate in class discussions
- Consider participating in Research Competitions‚Äîthese are highly valued in China and provide excellent experience
